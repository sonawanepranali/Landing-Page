Today Is "National Take Your Dog to work Day"

National Take Your Dog to Work Day is an annual event observed on the Friday after Father's Day, which falls on June 23 in 2023. This special day, established in 1999, aims to honor and promote adoption of companion canines. It provides an opportunity for employees to bring their furry friends to the workplace, fostering a positive and fun environment.
